The Vision

Not a Timeshare. Real Ownership.

El Nido is a new model for property ownership — one where a community of like-minded individuals holds real equity in a thriving tropical property, governed democratically through blockchain.

📈

Real Equity

You purchase shares of Thélème S.A., a Costa Rican corporation. Each share represents real, transferable equity — not a usage license. Your investment appreciates with the property.

🗳

Democratic Governance

Every decision is made by shareholders, enforced through blockchain. One share, one vote. Budget approvals, management changes, property improvements — the community decides together.

🌱

Living Community

Join a community of permaculture enthusiasts, remote workers, and nature lovers who are actively improving the property, expanding the food forest, and building something meaningful together.

💰

Rental Income

When you're not visiting, rent your unused nights on Airbnb, VRBO, Booking, or Expedia. Or use HomeExchange ($235/year) for free stays worldwide.

🍎

Eat from the Land

Our established food forest provides fresh tropical fruit year-round. Three aquaponics tanks raise tilapia, and a greenhouse grows vegetables. Self-sufficiency at its finest.

📍

Premium Location

20 minutes to pristine Pacific beaches. 45 minutes to Manuel Antonio — one of the world's most beautiful national parks. Walking distance to Selvatica eco-retreat. The best of jungle AND beach.

Governance

Democracy on the Blockchain

Every share is one vote. All decisions are transparent, recorded on-chain, and enforced by smart contracts.

👥

New Member Buy-In

Requires majority vote from existing members. The community chooses who joins.

📈

Share Price Appreciation

Share prices expected to rise ~10%/year, reflecting property improvements and market appreciation.

🗳

New Assessments

Require majority vote to approve. Once approved, assessments become mandatory for all members.

🛡

Member Removal

If a member is judged harmful: 67% vote triggers buyback at original purchase price.

🔧

Special Improvements

A member who makes improvements can request payment via majority vote, paid from HOA funds or special assessment.

⏰

Late Dues Enforcement

Late dues >1 year: balance paid from home equity with 20% premium using last approved share price. Fractional shares tracked to 2 decimals. Balance below zero = membership terminated.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a timeshare?
No. You purchase real shares in Thélème S.A., a Costa Rican corporation that owns the property. This is actual equity ownership — you can sell your shares, earn appreciation, and vote on all decisions.
How do points work?
Each share comes with 15 points per year. Nights cost 2–7 points depending on season. Your point balance can range from -10 to +10. Points above +10 don't roll over. You cannot book if your balance is below -10.
Can I rent out my nights?
Absolutely. You can list your unused nights on Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, or Expedia. You can also join HomeExchange ($235/year) to swap your nights for free stays at properties worldwide.
How is the property governed?
Democratically via blockchain. Every share equals one vote. New members require majority approval. New assessments require majority vote. A member judged harmful can be removed with a 67% vote and bought back at original purchase price.
What happens if I want to sell?
You can sell your shares at any time. Share transfers happen via blockchain with no government transfer tax. Share prices are expected to appreciate ~10% per year, so your investment grows over time.
What does the maintenance fee cover?
$100/share/year covers water supply, Starlink internet, monthly gardening, and basic repairs. Each owner pays their own electricity (metered) during stays. The total pool is $8,000/year across all 80 shares.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes! Rent El Nido for $50/night + $45 cleaning fee per stay (not per night). Experience the property, meet the community, and then decide if ownership is right for you.
What's the cancellation policy?
Cancel or modify free up to 1 month before your stay. Within 1 week of check-in, 50% of points are refunded. After that, points are not refundable unless you arrange a direct exchange with another owner.
Is there internet for remote work?
Yes. El Nido has Starlink high-speed satellite internet, making it fully digital-nomad ready. Work from the ocean-view deck with toucans flying by.
